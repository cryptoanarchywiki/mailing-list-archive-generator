Beginning Linux Programming

Inside Linux
Randolph Bentson
ISBN 0-916151-89-1
(mine was $22.00, no price shown)

ISBN 1-55828-507-5
$39.95

Linux Kernel Internals

The Design of the Unix Operating System
M.J. Bach
ISBN 0-13-201799-7
(no price on book, this is the book Linus used to design the kernel)

J.S. Gray
ISBN 0-13-186891-8
(no price on book, $40?)
