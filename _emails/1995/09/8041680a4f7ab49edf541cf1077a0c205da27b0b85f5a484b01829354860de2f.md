 % objdump -d netscape

 Disassembly of section .text:
 00002020 clr  %fp
 00002024 ld  [ %sp + 0x40 ], %o0
 00002028 add  0x44, %sp, %o1
 0000202c sll  %o0, 2, %o2
 00002030 add  4, %o2, %o2
 00002034 add  %o1, %o2, %o2
 . . .

This should work wherever other GNU utilities work.  objdump is part
of GNU binutils.
