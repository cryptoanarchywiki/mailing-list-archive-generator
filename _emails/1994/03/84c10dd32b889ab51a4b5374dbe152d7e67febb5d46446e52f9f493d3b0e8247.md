The April '94 "Special All-Fabio" issue of Software Development magazine
has a good article on the NII: "Highway to Hell?" by Alex Lane.  (He means
as in "paved with good intentions.")  Analysis of where it's likely to lead
given wording of various agenda documents and bills, statements of proponents,
and the history of various analogous developments (Interstates, space shuttles,
telephones, the census, the War on Poverty, ARPANET-->NREN-->NII...)
