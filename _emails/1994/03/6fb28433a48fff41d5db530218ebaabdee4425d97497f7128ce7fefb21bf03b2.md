"Anon-To: remailer@somewhere..."  Obviously someone is trying to chain 
their message through several remailers.  The problem is, my remailer

X-Anon-To: user@host
Request-Remailing-To: user@host

I feel like after 50 messages, I'm allowed to say I'm annoyed!
